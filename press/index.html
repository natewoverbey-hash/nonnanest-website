About Nonnanest

Nonnanest is the only baby monitor that senses skin temperature without touching your baby — no wearables, no WiFi, no subscriptions. Founded in Charleston, South Carolina, Nonnanest was born in a nursery, not a boardroom — by a parent who realized the market didn't need another camera, it needed awareness. Every other monitor that tracks wellness insights requires a wearable. Nonnanest closes that gap with patent-pending contactless infrared technology that gives parents continuous comfort awareness through a dedicated 5" HD handheld display, fully offline and private by design. The Nonnanest monitor is available for $329 as a one-time purchase with all features included.
