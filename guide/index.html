Safety warnings

Please read these warnings carefully before setting up your Nonnanest monitor. Your family's safety comes first.

Choking & strangulation hazard

Keep the power cable and all cords away from your baby at all times. A cable near the crib can pose a serious entanglement risk. Always route cables along walls and out of reach.

Minimum distance requirement

Keep the camera unit, power supply, and all cables at least 3 feet (1 meter) away from the crib mattress surface. Never place any part of the monitor inside the crib or bed.

Power & electrical safety

Use only the included power adaptor (DC 5V). Do not use third-party chargers or adaptors, as these may damage the device or create a safety hazard. Do not touch the plug with sharp or metal objects. Keep the monitor away from water, heaters, and humidifiers.

  • Do not expose either unit to extreme heat, humidity, or liquids.
  • Keep both units out of reach of children at all times.
  • Store this guide somewhere accessible for future reference.

Camera setup & mounting

Both mounting options are included. Choose the one that suits your nursery ‚Äî you can always switch later as your baby grows.

Choosing your mount

Flexible Crib Mount ‚Äî Perfect for newborns. Clips onto the crib rail and adjusts to position the camera directly above the mattress at the ideal downward angle.

Wall Mount Bracket ‚Äî Flush-mounted and out of reach, ideal once your baby can sit or pull to stand. Provides a permanent, secure install.

Placement tip

For the most accurate skin temperature readings, the camera should be positioned directly above the crib, facing downward. Before mounting, measure the distance from where the camera lens will sit to the mattress surface ‚Äî you'll use this in Step 5 (distance calibration).

Powering the camera

  1. Connect the USB-C power cable to the camera unit.
  2. Route the cable safely along the wall or crib frame, secured out of baby's reach.
  3. Plug the adaptor into a wall outlet.
  4. The camera status light will briefly illuminate, then go dark ‚Äî this is by design. No LEDs remain visible during normal use.

Pairing your display

The camera and display are factory pre-paired. Simply power on both devices and they will automatically connect over FHSS.

Press and hold the power button on the top of the handheld display for 3 seconds. The display will turn on and locate your camera within a few seconds.

Distance calibration

How to measure

Use a tape measure from the camera lens straight down to the mattress surface. Take note of this distance in feet or centimeters.

Selecting your setting

Skin temperature monitoring

Nonnanest uses a contactless infrared sensor to read your baby's skin temperature continuously, without any wearables or physical contact with your little one.

How it works

The infrared sensor in the camera measures the radiant heat from your baby's skin surface ‚Äî specifically from the forehead area as your baby lies in the crib. The reading is displayed in real time on the parent display alongside the room temperature.

üå°
Skin temperature vs. body temperature

Nonnanest measures skin surface temperature, which naturally varies with room conditions, clothing, and your baby's activity. Skin temperature readings are wellness indicators ‚Äî they help you spot patterns and shifts over time, not provide clinical measurements. Always consult your pediatrician if you have health concerns about your child.

Wellness alerts

The display will gently alert you when skin temperature patterns shift noticeably ‚Äî no manual threshold configuration is needed. When an alert appears, a visual indicator will show on screen. If sound alerts are enabled, a soft beep will sound and you can press any button to dismiss it.

These alerts are wellness cues to check on your baby ‚Äî not clinical diagnostics. If your baby's skin temperature reading seems elevated, check on them directly and contact your healthcare provider if you have concerns.

Getting accurate readings

  • Ensure distance calibration (Section 04) matches your actual camera height.
  • Position the camera directly above the crib, facing straight down.
  • Allow 10‚Äì15 minutes after room temperature changes for readings to stabilize.
  • Room air circulation, fans, and vents near the crib can affect readings ‚Äî minimize drafts directly over the crib.
  • The infrared sensor has a natural margin of ¬±1¬∞C / ¬±1.8¬∞F.

Settings & features

Access all settings via the Menu button (OK/M) on the handheld display. Use the directional arrows to navigate between options and OK to confirm.

Volume

Press the left or right directional arrows directly from the main view to adjust volume. Six levels are available (0‚Äì6). Audio from the camera is always transmitted ‚Äî volume only affects playback on the display.

Two-way audio (Intercom)

Press and hold the microphone button on the display to speak directly to your baby through the camera speaker. Release the button when you're done. This is a push-to-talk mode ‚Äî your voice transmits only while the button is held.

Lullabies

Press the music button or navigate to Menu ‚Üí Lullaby. Select an individual track or choose the "loop all" option to cycle through all lullabies continuously. Press the music button again to stop. Long-press the music button from the main screen to activate loop-all playback as a shortcut.

VOX / Sound-activated screen wake

VOX mode automatically wakes the display screen when the camera detects sound above a set threshold ‚Äî useful when you want the screen to stay dark until your baby stirs.

  • Navigate to Menu ‚Üí VOX and toggle on or off.
  • When VOX is on, the screen dims after 30 seconds of silence and wakes automatically when sound is detected. Audio is always transmitted even when the screen is off.
  • Short-press the menu key from the main screen to toggle VOX on/off as a shortcut.
  • When VOX is off, the screen stays illuminated continuously.

Display brightness

Press the brightness buttons (or navigate via Menu ‚Üí Brightness) to cycle through six brightness levels. Lowering brightness extends battery life and is useful for nighttime use without disturbing the bedroom.

Night vision

Night vision activates automatically when the camera's ambient light sensor detects a dark environment. The infrared LED illuminates the room invisibly, giving you a clear black-and-white image without disturbing your baby's sleep. You can also toggle it manually from the display.

Temperature units (¬∞F / ¬∞C)

Navigate to Menu ‚Üí Temperature ‚Üí Unit to switch between Fahrenheit and Celsius. This setting is saved and persists between sessions.

Feeding reminder

Navigate to Menu ‚Üí Alarm to set a recurring feeding reminder. Available intervals: 30 min, 1h, 2h, 3h, and 4h. When the reminder fires, the display will beep and a feeding icon will flash on screen. Press any button to dismiss.

Zoom

Navigate to Menu ‚Üí Zoom to select digital zoom levels. Use this to get a closer view of your baby without physically moving the camera.

Video recording (SD card)

Nonnanest supports local video recording to a microSD card (up to 32GB, FAT32 format). Insert the SD card into the slot on the camera unit. From the main screen, press and hold the center button for 1.5 seconds to start recording ‚Äî a flashing indicator will appear. Press and hold again to stop. Recorded video can be viewed by removing the SD card and playing it on a computer. Playback on the display is not supported.

System clock

Navigate to Menu ‚Üí Time to set the current time (hours, minutes, seconds). Use the directional arrows to adjust each value and confirm with OK.

Display orientation

If you mount the camera upside down for a particular installation angle, you can flip the display image. Navigate to Menu ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Orientation and select the correct rotation.

Troubleshooting & FAQ

My display isn't connecting to the camera
Ensure both the camera and display are powered on. The camera powers on automatically when plugged in ‚Äî the status light will briefly illuminate. The display requires a long press (3 seconds) of the power button. If they don't connect automatically, try manual pairing: hold the pairing button on both devices simultaneously for 5 seconds. If pairing fails repeatedly, try bringing both devices closer together (within 2 meters) to re-pair, then move them to their final positions.
The signal keeps dropping or the image is choppy
FHSS signal can be attenuated by reinforced concrete walls, large metal appliances (refrigerators, metal doors), and some electronic equipment. Try repositioning either unit to reduce the number of walls or obstructions between them. If a large appliance is between the camera and display, try a different path. Radios and other wireless devices can sometimes cause interference ‚Äî move the display away from these sources.
I'm hearing feedback / howling sounds
This happens when the display and camera are too close together or the display microphone is facing the camera speaker. Keep the display and camera at least 1.5 meters (5 feet) apart. Avoid pointing the display directly at the camera. This is most common when you bring the display into the nursery ‚Äî just move it to a doorway or adjacent room.
The skin temperature reading seems off
First, verify that your Distance setting (Section 04) matches your actual camera height above the mattress. The infrared sensor needs this calibration to be accurate. Also allow readings to stabilize for 10‚Äì15 minutes if the room temperature has changed recently. Fans, AC vents, or open windows near the crib can cause lower-than-expected skin temperature readings. The sensor has a natural margin of ¬±1¬∞C / ¬±1.8¬∞F. Remember that skin temperature naturally varies with room conditions and activity ‚Äî it is not equivalent to a rectal or oral temperature measurement.
The display screen isn't turning on
If VOX mode is active, the screen will stay dark until it detects sound from the camera. Press any button on the display to wake it manually. If the battery is low, a series of 5 beeps will sound ‚Äî plug in the display using the included power adaptor to charge. Hold the power button for 3 seconds to turn the display on from a fully off state.
Does Nonnanest require WiFi or an app?
No. Nonnanest operates entirely offline using FHSS (Frequency Hopping Spread Spectrum) encrypted radio. There is no WiFi, no cloud connection, no app, and no account. The camera and display communicate directly with each other on a dedicated, encrypted signal. Nothing leaves your home.
Can I use Nonnanest with multiple cameras?
The display supports pairing with multiple cameras. Navigate to Menu ‚Üí Camera ‚Üí Pair to add a second camera. You can then switch between camera views from the same menu, or enable cyclic mode to automatically rotate between cameras at a set interval. Note that one camera can only be paired to one display at a time.
How long does the display battery last?
Battery life depends on screen brightness and how often the screen is active. Using VOX mode (screen dims when quiet) significantly extends battery life. When battery is low, you'll hear 5 consecutive beeps as a reminder ‚Äî plug in the included power adaptor to charge. You can use the display while it's charging.

Care & maintenance

A little care goes a long way in keeping your Nonnanest performing well for years.

Cleaning

Clean both the camera and the handheld display with a slightly damp soft cloth. Do not use harsh chemicals, solvents, or abrasive cleaners ‚Äî these can damage the device surface and may void the warranty. Allow both units to dry completely before use.

Storage & environment

  • Keep both units away from direct water sources, steam, and high humidity environments.
  • Do not expose either unit to extreme heat ‚Äî keep away from radiators, space heaters, and direct sunlight.
  • Store in a cool, dry location when not in use.
  • Do not drop or subject either unit to physical shock.

Infrared lens care

The infrared lens on the camera is the key to accurate skin temperature readings. Keep it free of dust, fingerprints, and smudges. Clean gently with a dry microfiber cloth ‚Äî avoid touching the lens directly. Never use liquid cleaners near the lens opening.
