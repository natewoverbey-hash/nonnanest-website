Shipping

Last updated February 24, 2026

Free standard shipping on every order. No minimums, no codes, no surprises at checkout.

Where we ship

We currently ship to all 50 US states and Washington, DC. We do not yet ship internationally. If you're outside the US and interested in Nonnanest, reach out to support@nonnanest.com — we'd love to hear from you and we're working on expanding.

Shipping methods and timelines

Method Delivery Cost
Standard 5 – 7 business days Free
Expedited 2 – 3 business days $14.99

Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays. Timelines begin once your order has shipped, not when the order is placed.

Processing time

Orders are typically processed and shipped within 1 – 2 business days. During high-demand periods, processing may take up to 3 business days. You'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order ships.

Tracking your order

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with a tracking number. You can use this to track your package through the carrier's website. If you haven't received tracking information within 3 business days of placing your order, please contact us at support@nonnanest.com.

Shipping address

Please double-check your shipping address before completing your order. We are not responsible for orders shipped to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If you realize there's an error, contact us as soon as possible — if the order hasn't shipped yet, we can update it.

Lost or damaged packages

If your package appears lost or arrives damaged, contact us at support@nonnanest.com with your order number. We'll work with the carrier to locate your package or arrange a replacement. We're real people and we'll make it right.

P.O. boxes and APO/FPO addresses

We can ship to P.O. boxes and APO/FPO addresses via standard shipping. Expedited shipping is not available for these addresses. Delivery times may be longer than estimates above.

Questions?

Reach us at support@nonnanest.com. We respond quickly — usually within one business day.
